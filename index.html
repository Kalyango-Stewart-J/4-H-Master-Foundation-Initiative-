4-H CROWN MASTER FOUNDATION

The 4-H Crown Master Foundation is a worldwide effort to open a door of hope for people of all ages, training and equipping them to pursue their God-given purpose in life. The foundation continues to establish an array of effective educational services for both children and adults, ranging from reading programs, to trades and technology certifications, to higher education. The Crown Foundation team is a growing community of compassionate educators who are helping people every day, enabling them to discover what it means to live life by God's design. The foundation is advanced not only by dedicated workers, but also by contributors to the Crown Foundation which has been established to support ongoing development and expansion.

Who We Are

The 4-H Crown Master Foundation Initiative is a non-profit organization committed to supporting orphans and vulnerable children through the transformative power of the 4-H youth development model. Founded on the principles of Head, Heart, Hands, and Health, we provide a structured yet nurturing environment for personal growth.

We bridge the gap between potential and opportunity, ensuring that children without family support still have access to mentorship, education, and community belonging.

Our Vision

A society where every orphan is empowered with the skills, confidence, and character to build a sustainable future for themselves and their communities.
